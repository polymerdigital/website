Our Purpose

Polymer Digital is a small and effective Chicago based web design studio that exists to help business owners market their companies. With a no bullshit approach to process and a tight knit team of creative craftsmen we are able to identify and solve issues that are preventing you from growing.

Our Story

When we started out in late 2017 a few of us were working together at a web design agency. Collectively we shared a common goal: providing customers with the honest service they need to solve the problems they're having. The decision was made to be different from our competition. Since then we have formed a thoughtful team of professionals that delivers high quality web design while prioritizing customer and team happiness over buzzwords, deadlines and budget.
