Story

Polymer Digital is a small and effective Chicago based web design studio that exists to help business owners market their companies. With a no bullshit approach to process and a tight knit team of creative craftsmen we are able to identify and solve issues that are preventing you from growing.

When we started out in late 2017 most of us were working together at a different web design agency. Collectively we shared a common goal: providing customers with the honest service they need to solve the problems they're having. The decision was made to be different than our agency peers; a thoughtful team of professionals that delivers high quality web design while prioritizing customer happiness over buzzwords, deadlines and budget.
